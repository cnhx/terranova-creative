About

Terranova Creative is an independent cultural strategy and institutional advisory practice based in California.

The firm works with arts organizations, cultural institutions, and globally oriented partners on structural planning, institutional positioning, and cross-border collaboration frameworks.

Its approach is research-driven, institutionally grounded, and internationally informed.

Advisory Scope

Institutional Strategy & Organizational Development

Long-term planning, structural alignment, and partnership frameworks for arts and cultural institutions.

Cross-Border Institutional Collaboration Advisory

Strategic guidance for collaboration between artists, museums, foundations, and international cultural partners.

Strategic Market Entry Advisory

Cultural alignment and institutional partnership assessment for globally operating brands engaging with the U.S. arts landscape.

Digital Audience & Experience Strategy

Research-based advisory for digital platform structuring and institutional audience development.

Cultural Media & Educational Initiative Development

Strategic development of cultural content and educational programming frameworks supporting institutional growth.
